We have a collection of 2920 Electronic books from Springer Publisher's Engineering and Technology Package. The Central Library is fully automated using KOHA Integrated Library System. KOHA ILS @ CEK Library provides OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) and the same is available throughout our campus wide LAN.
